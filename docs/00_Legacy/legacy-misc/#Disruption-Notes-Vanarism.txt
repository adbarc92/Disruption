Incongruous Creed, Unknowable Truth, Inarticulable Truth,

Inverse Encapsulation: truth/reality cannot be encapsulated in any creedal form

Multiple Truths

Sanatana Dharma: the eternal or absolute set of duties or religiously ordained practices incumbent upon all Hindus

Four Noble Truths:
	* Life involves suffering, duhkha
	* Suffering is caused by desire
	* There is a way out of suffering
	* That way is the Noble Eightfold Path

Dharma: righteousness, ethics

Artha: livelihood, wealth

Kaama: sensual pleasure

Moksa: liberation

Karma: action, work, deed

Moksha: ultimate goal of life, Nirvana

Forms:

Hand of Knives: finger bones become thicker, longer, and sharper, extending through the skin
Serpent's Fangs: arms become long tentacles with "mouths" for grasping
Heavy Fist: bone-plating along the knuckles for maximum blunt force
Unravel: become like a squid
