## Inventory

### Items

Items can be used both in-combat and in the field. Players should feel (healthy) pressure to use items.

### Ampoules

Ampoules are the premium currency of this world. They are used to power utility Devices within the City and will recharge within the Forest (TODO: rate?) during both traversal and combat. They can be exchanged for powerful items. The magical charge they capture can be spent to recharge Devices out in the Forest, or to power combat Devices within the City.

## Social Connections & Dialogue

### Pain Points
- Mass Effect - Binary, punishes nuance or lack of commitment
- Persona - Just use a guide. Time + optimal path = catastrophe

### Points of Reference
- Deus Ex: Human Revolution - You must read people.

### Our Approach

1. The Triangle - There are at least three different approaches to responses (roughly inspired by OCEAN, but simpler): 
- Bold: Direct, honest, dominant
- Socratic: Questioning, curious, insightful
- Kind: Loving, patient, understanding
